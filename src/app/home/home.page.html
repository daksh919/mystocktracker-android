About MyStockTracker


Investing for the long term often involves monitoring stock prices diligently, especially for opportunities to buy more shares when prices decline. However, tracking stocks daily can be time-consuming and challenging, particularly when aiming to capitalize on specific price drops, such as a 20% decrease.

Our platform aims to streamline this process and alleviate the burden of constant monitoring. Imagine wanting to purchase shares of a stock when it experiences a total of 20% drop in upcoming days, but the stock's price fluctuates daily, making it difficult to pinpoint the opportune moment.

Here's where our solution comes in: Upon subscribing to our service, users can effortlessly set alerts for desired stock price drops. For instance, if you're interested in Reliance stock and set a 20% drop alert, our system will continuously monitor the stock's performance for you.

Instead of manually checking stock prices every day, our application will notify you via email when the stock reaches your predefined threshold. This allows you to stay informed and act decisively without the hassle of constant vigilance.

Key Features

  • Customizable Alerts: Tailor alerts based on your specific investment criteria, whether it's a percentage drop, specific price point, or other parameters relevant to your strategy.
  • Portfolio Tracking: Keep track of your investments effortlessly with our portfolio tracking tools, enabling you to monitor performance and make informed decisions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a user-friendly interface designed for seamless navigation and intuitive use, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.
